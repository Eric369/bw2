SCUBA diving the Fort Lauderdale area and the Florida Keys. Here we are your guides to diving the blue wild.

If you've found yourself here and are looking to do some scuba diving, you found the right place. We teach PADI courses from Discover Scuba Diving, Open Water all the way to Divemaster and specialities. Located in Hollywood, Florida makes us centrally located to the South Florida diving. While we mostly scuba dive the Fort Lauderdale area, we often dive out of Pompano Beach as well as the Florida Keys.

Need to take a scuba refresher course? Looking for a divemaster guide? How about a beach dive? Want to continue your scuba education or perhaps become a PADI Divemaster? We are a husband / wife small business and there's a lot we can do in regards to your SCUBA diving adventures. Check us out and feel free to contact us with any questions.
